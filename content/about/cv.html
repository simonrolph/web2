Conferences, Courses and Working Groups

December 2017 Ecology Across Borders (British Ecological Soceity Annual Meeting) in Ghent. I presented a poster on my work on simulated plant life history strategies as a tool to guide comparative research of matrix population models.
March 2017 Matrix Approaches Health Demography (2 weeks), taught by Hal Caswell at the Max Planck Institute for Demographic Research. This course introduced methods based on Markov chains, Markov chains with rewards, multistate matrix models, and matrix calculus.
December 2016 British Ecological Society Annual Meeting in Liverpool. Presented an outreach poster about the COMPADRE Plant Matrix Database and COMADRE Plant Matrix Dabase. A version of this poster is available here.
October 2016 Integral Projection Models: Demography in a Continuous World (1 week), Barcelona. Taught by Tim Coulson, Eelke Jongejans, Rob Salguero-Gómez and Maria Paniw. This course was coordinated by Transmitting science. This course taught basic and advanced applications of IPMs to construct IPMs and estimate a number of important population rates including sensitivity analyses, life table response experiments, stochastic projections and times to local extinction.
June 2016 International Demography Working Group (1 week), hosted by Prof. Dave Hodgson at the University of Exeter. Other attendees were population ecologists from around the world: Elizabeth Crone, Drew Tyre, Judy Che-Castaldo, Iain Stott, Rob Salguero-Gómez, Owen Jones, Ruth Archer, Yvonne Buckley.
